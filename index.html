IEEEXtreme Registration

Welcome to IEEEXtreme!

IEEEXtreme is an annual global programming competition where IEEE student members compete in a 24-hour time span against each other to solve a set of programming challenges. Ready to showcase your coding skills?

Instructions to Register

  1. IEEE Membership: Ensure that you are a valid IEEE student member. If not, register at the official IEEE website.
  2. Team Formation: Form a team of up to 3 members. All team members should be IEEE student members.
  3. Proctor Selection: Choose a proctor who is an IEEE member (faculty or professional) to supervise your team during the competition. Below is the list of available proctors:
