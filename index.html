Welcome to IEEEXtreme 18.0!

IEEEXtreme is an annual global programming competition where IEEE student members compete in a 24-hour time span to solve challenging problems. This guide is specifically for students of BMSIT to help you register and participate in the event successfully. Follow the steps below to register your team.

Steps to Register

  1. Ensure IEEE Membership: All participants must be IEEE student members. If you're not yet a member, please register on the IEEE official website.
  2. Form a Team: Create a team of up to 3 members. Make sure all team members have valid IEEE memberships.
